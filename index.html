I did not start ElevateX to build another agency. I started it because I kept seeing brilliant founders, business owners, and operators with incredible products and services, but no system to get them in front of the right people.

I have been in the trenches. I was Global Marketing Manager for a fintech company, managing a six figure monthly ad budget and leading a team of 10 across paid media, content, and partnerships. I have consulted for over 40 businesses spanning SaaS, education, e-commerce, construction, healthcare, and professional services. I built the entire go to market strategy for Athena Edge, a prop trading firm, growing it from an idea to a 45,000 person email list and a thriving community of over 5,000 traders.

Through all of it, the pattern was always the same. Founders and business owners with real vision, real expertise, and a product worth talking about, but no system to turn attention into revenue. They were either doing everything manually, burning money on disconnected tools, or paying agencies who delivered nothing but vanity metrics in a PDF.

That is why ElevateX exists. I take your vision and I execute it. I become part of your team. I build the ads, the funnels, the automation, the AI, the CRM, the entire growth engine. Not as an outsider looking in, but as someone who is genuinely invested in seeing your business succeed.

I only take on businesses I believe in. If we are not the right fit, I will tell you on the call. No games, no pressure. Just an honest conversation between two people who care about building something real.
